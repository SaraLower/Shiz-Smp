Characters

Scruffy (Cj)

Scruffy is an ancestor of an ancient race of snow golems, and as a result he has a pumkin color pallet and is made of a snow like substance. He has no arms and uses his mouth to pick up and use objects. Scruffy wears a green & orange striped scarf and has a stump growing out of his head. He often wears a suit with a red and white striped tie. Scruffy is on of the richest players withen the server. He owns the Scruffy Spy Network (SSN) and he is the king of Scruff City. Scruffy once was possessed by a pumkin demon. This pumpkin demon went by the name "The Seedler" and would not stop until everyone was a pumpkin.

Shizard (Teah)

Shizard is a demon that was sent down from heaven to the Shiz Smp. Shizard has a huge circular mouth that makes up most of their face, pale skin, and dark black hair. They can also swap between their Demon form and their human form. Their human form has tan hair, magma feet, and a green hoodie. Shizard consists of a flesh only diet and will steal any flesh that you have, giving them flesh will earn their trust. They live in a hobbit hole in the ground in the cherry blossom section of Shiz Zentral, and is also the main founder of Shiz Zentral, the second largest country in the Shiz Smp in population and landmass. Shizard has three pets, Darbie, Milo, and Cadaver. Milo tragically died due to a freak accident involving a rabid goat., and Darby died in a scuffle between a drowned and Shizard and Darby was unfortunately caught in the crosshairs.

Sara

Sara is a bipedal llama who originally was a resident of Shiz Zentral, but she later created her own nation, JLC (Jungle Lion Castle), to worship and protect her parrot, Jungle Lion. Sara is the owner of the largest dungeon so far and is a powerful member of the Shiz Smp. Sara will do anything for Jungle Lion and will destroy Jungle Lion haters at all costs. Sara also has over 50 dogs that she slaughters when convenient.

Milo

Milo is a Florian who enjoys all things plants and nature. Milo is a recent addition to the Shiz Smp as of January 7th, 2024 and he already is making great strides in the Shiz Smp world. Milo ventured off and built a city off of the remains of EnderKatichus cherry blossom house's remains and has turned it into his own country named "Cherry Grove City". He has a pet bird named Kiwi and is one of the most active members of the Shiz Smp.

Finch (Cole)

Finch Maurice Parker, also known as Finch for short, is a human who may or may not have spider like abilities. Finch is a recent addition to the Shiz Smp as of Jan 21st, 2024. Finch wears a brown jacket with a white shirt underneath and sports steam punk like goggles with a star that floats around his head as an accessory. Finch also wears a white scarf around his neck, and this has caused Scruffy to have high respect for Finch since Scruffy has much love for people who wear scarves. Finch is a star keeper, meaning that he can harness stars and have them as friends, the star that floats around his head is one of them and is named Rory. Finch is slowly creating a parrot army, each named after something space related.
